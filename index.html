The Story

It was a small job given to me by a mentor that helped me realize I wanted to join ICC’s media team. It was a text from a peer that encouraged me to push forward on a goal I had been struggling to accomplish. And it was a conversation with a student leader I looked up to that inspired me to want to be as mature and Christ-like as him when I got older. I’ve never forgotten those small, impactful moments, and this summer, I will be joining ICC’s student Ambassador team. We will be hosting events in the US as well as in SouthEast Asia and West Africa. This is an amazing opportunity that will not only grow me, but will allow me to set an example for others and show them the love of Christ. I hope to make an impact on others the way so many other students have made an impact on me. I want to thank you for considering supporting me and my team as we show others the love of Christ.

~ Josiah
